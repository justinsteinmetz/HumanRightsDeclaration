Human Rights

You benefit from systems
that violate them.

"Not me."

Yes, you.

You didn't build it.

You live inside it.

Clothes. Phones. Food.

Convenience has a cost.

Human rights are sustained by systems.

You decide.

One right is removed.

```
Freedom of speech
Privacy
Fair trial
Education
```

It is now gone.

At first… nothing changes.

You accept it.

Then it reaches you.

For a moment…

it feels like change.

You watch.
You scroll.

You continue.
