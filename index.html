1948

After World War II, the world faced one question:

How do you stop power from abusing people?

“Everyone has rights.”

That was the claim.

Do you believe this?

All humans have the same rights
Rights cannot be taken away
Everyone is treated equally

So what are these “rights”… really?

Freedom of speech
Education
No torture
A fair trial

Now be honest.

These rights exist…

Do they actually exist in reality?

A government passes a law.

Some people can be executed.

Others cannot.

And it’s legal.


Is that justice… or power?

“So what actually stops this?”

You decide.

A government can remove ONE right to keep control.

No talking. Decide alone.

Freedom of speech
Privacy
Fair trial
Education

Which one do you remove?

Write one sentence.

This is what happens.

Rights don’t enforce themselves.

They exist only if someone defends them.

If no one stops it…
were those rights ever real?
