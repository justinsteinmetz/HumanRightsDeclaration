Human Rights

You benefit from systems
that violate them.

You're thinking:

"Not me."

Yes, you.

You didn't build it.

You didn't design it.

You live inside it.

Clothes.

Phones.

Food.

Convenience has a cost.

Human rights are not just violated by governments.

They are sustained by systems.

1948

After World War II, the world made a promise.

"Never again."

Human rights exist.

They are written down.

They are agreed on.

They are not guaranteed.

“Everyone has rights.”

That’s the claim.

Not the reality.

Rights are written down.

Power decides when they matter.

Those are not the same thing.

For a moment…

it feels like change.

You watch.
You scroll.

You continue.
